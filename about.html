Our Story

Rooted in Amalner, Built on Trust

Since Our Founding

A Legacy Carried Forward

Paras Gold began with a simple belief: that jewellery should carry meaning, not just metal. What started as a small showroom in Amalner has grown into a name families trust for life's most golden moments — weddings, festivals, and everyday celebrations.

Every piece we craft reflects generations of skill, an unwavering commitment to purity, and a deep respect for the customers who have walked through our doors for years.

Our Identity

The Meaning Behind Our Mark

PG

At the center, the P and G don't just stand together — they orbit each other. This is balance: creation and refinement, matter and meaning, the visible and the felt. The inner sparkle is not decoration — it's the point of awakening, the exact moment metal stops being an object and becomes identity, memory, emotion.
